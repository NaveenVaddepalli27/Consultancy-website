Interim CXO Search

Often do companies in today’s ever-changing economic landscape invariably in situations emerging out of rapid growth, M&A, departure of a key executive with no successor in visibility or to solve a specific business problem need a specific leadership skill for a defined duration.In similar situations 3P is geared up to respond with equal sense of urgency and come up with an ‘Interim CXO’ in the shortest of timeframe, often days if not hours.

Case Study 1:
Client - Global Leader in Hydraulic Systems

Need : Our client faced unprecedented crisis in their Indian supply chain especially in area of procurement leading to a delay in supply of finished goods to their key customers. This circumstance led the Head of Supply Chain resign at a short notice.

Solution : 3P quickly identified and positioned a very senior procurement professional from the automobile industry who was prompt to take full control of the crisis like situation and instil confidence amongst their global customers.

Case Study 2:
Client - Global Leader in Construction Materials

Need : The Company was undergoing a restructuring of their business division with significant global intervention. Their India Human Resources Head quit during this period, effecting the implementation of the same.

Solution : 3P was retained to find an Interim Manager with strong operational experience. In this case a self- employed consultant was identified for this role and did the task exceptionally well and in turn was absorbed as their full time Human Resources Director.

Case Study 3:
Client - Global Consumer Goods Major

Need : Our client approached us in a near crisis like situation having terminated the services of their CFO at a very short notice owing to non-adherence and non-compliance.

Solution : 3P advisory service were sought to manage the exit of this incumbent and an interim CFO was positioned within 24 hours of this departure. This interim CFO performed exceedingly well and was retained as an advisor even after the recruitment of a full time CFO.
