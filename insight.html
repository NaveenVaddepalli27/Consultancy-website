SEARCH PROCESS

STEP
1

PLANNING
  • Structured Client Briefing
  • Understand Profile, Competencies and Assessment Criterias
  • Agree to the Search Strategy & timelines
  • Design Candidate Assessment Matrix

STEP
2

RESEARCH & IDENTIFICATION
  • Implement Research
  • Target Industries, Companies & Functions
  • Networking & Sourcing
  • Identify Potential Prospects

STEP
3

ASSESSMENT
  • Engage Prospects
  • Evaluate against functional assessment criteria
  • Assessment of Interpersonal Skills, Managerial / Leadership Capabilities
  • Conclude Interviews and prepare shortlist

STEP
4

RECOMMENDATION
  • Prepare Candidate Assessment Reports
  • Present the shortlisted candidates
  • Co-ordinate interviews
  • Discuss Interview

STEP
5

APPOINTMENT
  • Facilitate Offer rollout & negotiation
  • Final Reference Check
  • Pre and post-Employment assistance
  • Search Closure
