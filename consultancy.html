ABOUT US

"A Leading Consulting Organization Driven by Knowledge, Integrity and Performance Committed to Client's success"

Consultants established in 1990, is India's premier retained executive search firm with presence in Mumbai, Pune & New Delhi and ranked amongst the Top 5 search firms of India. Our commitment to client satisfaction, deep regard for personal relationships and dedication to quality is the cornerstone to our continued success. Our clients often refer to us as a “Trusted Advisor” and we take this to be the highest compliment. Being candid about our qualifications is one of our biggest strengths and clients value the discretion, experience and judgement we bring as consultants to their search for leaders.
